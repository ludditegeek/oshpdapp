Preventing Page Footer Float

By default page footers float up on the page - how far depends on the amount of content on the page. Often this is not what you want and getting it to look just right can drive you nuts. You're 90% done (you think) and it takes another day or two to tweak things to perfection. On some of my (shorter) pages I found that the page footer was creeping up to the middle of the page when I really want it pinned to the bottom of the screen. Apparently footer creep is the default behaviour. You can force the footer to the bottom of a page by adding the following javascript function to your page. (there may better ways that I don't know of). Here's a useful link with more info.
